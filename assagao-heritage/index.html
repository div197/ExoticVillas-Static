Assagao Heritage, Goa

Inspired by traditional Portuguese architecture, the villa is a fine blend of the traditional and the contemporary. The resplendent interiors combine tastefully with the old-world elegance to reflect the rich cultural heritage of Goa. The villa is located in a quaint and beautiful neighbourhood, minutes away from the beaches, some of the most amazing restaurants, boutiques and night life. Three immaculately decorated, air-conditioned luxury rooms recall the grandiose & glory of a bygone era. Four well appointed, air-conditioned rooms with a new age twist are perfect for family getaways. A swimming pool with a breath-taking scenic beauty and a unique ambience takes the centre stage. We are kid-friendly and pets are allowed. Barbeque, live singing and village walks are experiences that are available on request and are chargeable but are worth a try as these are etched in memory forever.Â

Facilities

The villa includes seven air-conditioned, well-equipped bedrooms with attached bathrooms, two living rooms, two lounge areas with a flat screen TV & cable connection, a study, one dining-area, a kitchen with pantry and a powder room. It boasts of a beautiful garden, an outdoor swimming pool, a gazebo and extras like car parking space, power backup and free Wi-Fi. A safety deposit box, bed & bath linen, hairdryer, electric kettle and built-in wardrobes. Our chargeable services include dining-in and ironing facilities.
